Getting involved

Edinburgh Pamphlet




What's up. Welcome to the website for the Edinburgh Pamphlet, a progressive politics and art publication based in — yep — Edinburgh. Here you can find past issues of the pamphlet, including versions of the articles we've published that include citations. Yay.

If you've read the pamphlet and think you may have something to contribute to a future issue we'd love to hear from you. Articles, short fiction, poetry, artwork — if you've got em, we're interested. For more info, check out the 'Getting Involved' page.
